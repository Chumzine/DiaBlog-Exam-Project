About DiaBlog

DiaBlog is an online space where you get to share things about your life, more like a diary but a public one. Do I hear you say, "How can I share my private and intimate things?" Fret not, because it doesn't hasve to be that private, just things about you that you will normally share on social media or tell friends.

With DiaBlog, you don't have to lug about a conventional diary and pen. You can document your life in a blog. So welcome to the world of blogging. You get to be a lifestyle blogger and share your life's stories with a community.

DiaBlog is created by Adaobi Chuma-Okeke, a Backend Engineering student of AltSchool Africa, using the HTML and CSS markup languages, and the Python programming language with Flask framework. You can connect with me via my Instagram and Twitter handles. I would love to hear from you.
